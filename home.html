Learn more about wrinkle phases before getting started:

Mild Wrinkles

Fine lines that appear only with facial expressions. Common in younger skin. Often reversible with hydration and SPF.

Moderate Wrinkles

Visible at rest, not just during facial movement. Often caused by aging, sun damage, or dehydration. Treatable with retinoids and barrier care.

Severe Wrinkles

Deep and permanent lines. Typically found in mature skin. Requires intensive anti-aging routines and consistent care.
